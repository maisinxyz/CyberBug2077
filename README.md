# Cyberbug 2077

**Welcome to the codeworld.** In **Cyberbug 2077**, you play as an anomally running around in a inifite codewworld. Your goal is simple: cause as much dismay (Corruption) as possible. At the same time make sure you're aware of enemy debuggers! While you're running around increasing corruption, debuggers try to hunt you down, and stop your destruction. 

---

## How do I play?

The codeworld is infinitely generating, meaning every single landscape you enter is different than the last. As you jump across platforms and dodge bottomless glitches, you'll need to interact with the environment to increase your corruption.


### Gameplay Mechanics

**Corruption & Multipliers (Your Score)**
    * Your score is called **Corruption**. You earn it by interacting with the environemnt, by breaking loop jails, incrementing variable values and calling function doors. 
      **Multiplier:** Keep an eye out for floating gold Coins (`×M`)! Grabbing these coins temporarily multiplies your corruption score, stacking up to 5x. Combine your parkour skills to grab coins all while breaking loops to gain the maximum corruption score!
      
  **Invisibility & Stealth**
     Debuggers are constantly tracking your digital footprint. To lose them, grab the floating purple orbs (`👁 INVIS`). 
     This cloaks you in invisibility, preventing drones from scanning you. If you collect multiple invisibility orbs in a row, you'll "tier up" your cloak—eventually allowing you to completely stun the drones!
    
**Warp Doors / Function Pads**
    * If you ever find yourself in a sticky situation, dont fret! You'll find purple `f()` warp doors. Run into them and press R to teleport to narrowly escape the debuggers!
    
**The Heat System**
    * The more chaos you cause, the more the system notices. The better you do, the more "Heat" you have,attracting more debuggers on your trail. Manage your heat by using stealth and collecting invisibility orbs.

## How do I play?

You only need 5 keys, ←, ↑  →, shift and R!

| Action | Controls | What it does |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Move** | `←` / `→` or `A` / `D` | Run left or right through the world. |
| **Jump** | `↑`, `W`, or `Space` | Leap over gaps. (Hold to jump slightly higher). |
| **Dash** | `Shift` or `E` | A quick dash to evade scanner beams and jump from platform to platform . |
| **Return Warp** | `R` or `Q` | Snap back to your original spot after using a Warp Door. |
| **Pause** | `P` or `Esc` | Pause. |

---

## Obstacle Quick Guide

Not everything in the city is trying to kill you. Use the code blocks to your advantage:

 * **[x=1] Variable Nodes:** Run through these cyan blocks repeatedly to score points and move nearby platforms to jump on.
 * **for(;;)** **Loop Jails:** If you run into this red zone, you are trapped! Jump into the glowing red core in the center to break the loop and get a boost your corruption.
* **Null Voids:** Dark gaps in the ground. If you fall in, you won't die, but you'll lose points and be stunned for a period, leaving you exposed to the debuggers.
